Acknowledgement of Country

This website was developed on the lands of the Yugara and Turrubal peoples, the traditional owners of much of Meanjin (Brisbane). Kira recognises that Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander sovereignty never ceded, the ongoing impacts of the forced disposition of Indigenous Australians off country, and the removal of over 100,000 children from family and community. I recognise the importance of reconcilliation, and the necessity of working together to improve the social and health outcomes of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.
